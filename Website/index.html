About the Game

Space Odyssey is a game which takes part in the vast space. There rules the empire, a powerful gonverment which has power over the entire galaxy. You are a betrayed space soldier, who is trying to survive and have your revenge against the empire which forsake you. You have to fight against powerful foes and make your way to the top of the empire. Are you ready to take on the challenge?
